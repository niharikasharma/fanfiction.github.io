The Interactive Fan Fiction Data Visualisation Guide

As of November 2016, FanFiction.net has 1.5 million active users on the portal. Since its launch in 1998, they have written more than 6.5 million stories in aggregate. Contesting in 44 different languages, an approximate of 10 thousand fandoms are written on a daily basis. We have decided to focus on the aspect of distributed mentorship between authors on this data rich platform.
